Whitesburg Tide Swim & Dive Teams

Any area youth from age 4 to 17 are invited to join the Whitesburg Tide swim and dive teams! We're part of the Rocket City Swim and Dive League and swimmers and divers of any skill level are welcome. For our younger swimmers, we even offer the Tiny Tides group for children ages 3-6 who are still working on basic swimming skills. Whether you're super competitive or just learning, our awesome coaches will have you setting PRs in no time... all while having fun with your friends at the pool!

Fees

Swim team fees include one t-shirt per swimmer. Dive fees do not include a t-shirt. Swim caps and extra shirts can be purchased at an additional cost if desired.

If you aren't a full pool member, you'll need to purchase a swim/dive team membership in addition to the team fees shown below. You may also consider getting a trial membership instead, which will allow your whole family to come to the pool all summer long.

Swim Team

  • 1 swimmer: $130
  • 2 swimmers: $210
  • 3 swimmers: $280
  • Each additional swimmer after 3: $15

Tiny Tides

  • $100 per swimmer

Dive Team

  • 1 diver: $90
  • 2 divers: $115
  • 3+ divers: $135

Swim/Dive Team-only Pool Membership

$150
